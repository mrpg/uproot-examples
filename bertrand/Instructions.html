You are matched with another participant. You will each independently and simultaneously choose a price to set (between 0 and {{ C.MAX_PRICE }}).

The demand at a given price is:

Q  =  100 − P

Price (P) Demand (Q = 100 − P)
$0100 units
$2575 units
$5050 units
$7525 units
$1000 units

The participant who sets the lower price captures all demand. If both set the same price, demand is split equally. There are no production costs.

Your earnings ($) = your price ($) × your demand (units).

You may use the simulator to explore different scenarios before making your decision.
