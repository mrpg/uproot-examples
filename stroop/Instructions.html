In this task, you will see color words (like Red, Blue, Green, Yellow) displayed in different font colors.

Your task: Identify the font color of the word, not the word itself.

Examples

Red → Correct answer: Red (word and color match)

Red → Correct answer: Blue (ignore the word, focus on the font color)

Located around the word, you will see several buttons with color names.

A correct response is made by pressing the button that names the font color of the word.

Examples

Yellow Red Blue Green

For Red, the correct response is pressing the Red button, since the word is displayed in red.

For Red, the correct response is pressing the Blue button, since the word is displayed in blue.

Respond as quickly and accurately as possible.
