Instructions

In this task, you will see color words (like RED, BLUE, GREEN, YELLOW) displayed in different ink colors.

Your task: Identify the ink color of the word, not the word itself.

Examples

RED → Correct answer: red (word and color match)

RED → Correct answer: blue (ignore the word, focus on the ink color)

Respond as quickly and accurately as possible.

Click the button matching the ink color you see.
