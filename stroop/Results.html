Stroop effect {{ results.stroop_effect | fmtnum(post=" ms", places=1) }}

The Stroop effect is the difference in the mean response time between correct reponses in incongruent trials and correct responses in congruent trials. A positive value indicates interference from the word meaning.

A second measure is the difference in the share of correct reponses in congruent trials and correct responses in incongruent trials. A larger share of correct responses in congruent trials (i.e., larger share of errors in incongruent trials) indicates interference from the word meaning.
