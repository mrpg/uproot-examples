In this task, you will evaluate 4 lotteries. Each lottery offers a 50–50 chance of winning one of two amounts. All lotteries have the same expected value of €50.

Lottery Outcomes (50–50) Expected value
L1 €47 or €53 €50
L2 €40 or €60 €50
L3 €25 or €75 €50
L4 €0 or €100 €50

For each lottery, you will answer 5 questions. Each question asks whether you prefer a specific certain amount or the lottery. Your answers determine how much each lottery is worth to you.

There are 20 questions in total.
