In this task, you can win a bonus of $100 if you correctly guess the outcome of a six-sided die roll.

On the next screen, you will be asked to think of a number between 1 and 6 that you believe the die will show. Your guess is completely private — it will not be written down or recorded in any way. It is impossible for anyone, including the researchers, to know what number you thought of.

After that, you will roll an electronic die on screen. You will then report the number you saw and state whether it was the number you had in mind.

If you guessed correctly, you will be eligible for a bonus payment of $100.
