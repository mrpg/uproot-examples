Your group of {{ C.GROUP_SIZE }} is deciding whether to fund a shared project that costs {{ C.COST | fmtnum(pre="$", places=2) }}.

If funded, every group member benefits — but each person benefits by a different amount. Your personal benefit from the project is {{ player.true_value | fmtnum(pre="$", places=2) }}. Only you know this number. The other members have their own personal benefits, which only they know.

The rules
  1. Report a value. Each group member privately reports how much the project is worth to them. You may report any amount from $0 to {{ C.MAX_VALUE | fmtnum(pre="$", places=0) }}.
  2. The decision. All reported values are added up. If the total is at least {{ C.COST | fmtnum(pre="$", places=2) }}, the project is funded. Otherwise, it is not.
  3. Your payment. You pay something only if the project would not have been funded without you — that is, if the other members' reports alone do not cover the cost. In that case, you pay just the gap: {{ C.COST | fmtnum(pre="$", places=2) }} minus what the others reported. If the others' reports already cover the cost, you pay nothing.
  4. Your earnings.
    • Project funded: your personal benefit minus your payment.
    • Project not funded: $0.
