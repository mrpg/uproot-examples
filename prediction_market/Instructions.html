Contracts

You start with an endowment of {{ C.ENDOWMENT | fmtnum(pre="$", places=2) }}. You can use this money to buy two types of contracts:

  • A Yes contract pays {{ 1 | fmtnum(pre="$", places=2) }} if the event occurs, and nothing otherwise.
  • A No contract pays {{ 1 | fmtnum(pre="$", places=2) }} if the event does not occur, and nothing otherwise.

The Automated Market Maker

Contract prices are set by an automated market maker. Prices always sum to {{ 1 | fmtnum(pre="$", places=2) }}.

The more contracts of one type are bought, the higher their price becomes. This means that early traders get a better price, but also take on more risk.

Trading

You can buy and sell contracts at any time. When you sell contracts you hold, you receive the current market price.

Your Payoff

Payoff = remaining cash + payout from contracts you hold

Try to maximize your payoff by trading wisely.
