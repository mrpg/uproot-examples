You are matched with another participant. You will each choose how many units to produce (between 0 and {{ C.MAX_UNITS }}), but sequentially: one participant decides first, then the other observes that choice and decides.

The market price is determined by the total number of units both of you produce together:

P  =  100 − Qtotal

Total units (Qtotal) Price (P)
0$100
25$75
50$50
75$25
100$0

Your earnings ($) = Price ($) × your units. There are no production costs.

{% endif %}
