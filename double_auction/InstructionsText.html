This is a double auction market where buyers and sellers can trade with each other. Trades occur when buyers and sellers agree on a price.

Your role

{% if player.buyer %}

You are a buyer.

Your valuation is ${{ player.cost_or_value | to(2) }}. This is the maximum value the good has to you.

If you trade, your profit is your valuation minus the price of the accepted offer. Mathematically, Profit = Valuation − Price.

{% else %}

You are a seller.

Your cost is ${{ player.cost_or_value | to(2) }}. This is the minimum price you need to cover your costs.

If you trade, your profit is the price of the accepted offer minus your cost. Mathematically, Profit = Price − Cost.

{% endif %}

If you don't trade, your profit is $0.

How to trade

Making an offer:

  • {% if player.buyer %}As a buyer, you can make a bid—this is the price you are willing to pay.{% else %}As a seller, you can make an ask—this is the price you are willing to accept.{% endif %}
  • Enter your desired price and click "Submit new offer".
  • You can change or cancel your offer at any time before someone accepts it.

Accepting an offer:

  • {% if player.buyer %}Look at the Asks section to see what sellers are willing to accept.{% else %}Look at the Bids section to see what buyers are willing to pay.{% endif %}
  • Click any offer to immediately trade at that price.
  • Once you trade, you cannot make any more offers this round.
