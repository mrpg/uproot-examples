How to trade

{% if can_offer %}

Making an offer:

  • {% if player.buyer %}As a buyer, you can make a bid—this is the price you are willing to pay.{% else %}As a seller, you can make an ask—this is the price you are willing to accept.{% endif %}
  • Enter your desired price and click "Submit new {% if player.buyer %}bid{% else %}ask{% endif %}".
  • You can change or cancel your offer at any time before someone accepts it.

Accepting an offer:

  • {% if player.buyer %}Look at the Asks section to see what sellers are willing to accept.{% else %}Look at the Bids section to see what buyers are willing to pay.{% endif %}
  • Click any offer to immediately trade at that price.
  • Once you trade, you cannot make any more offers this round.
{% else %}

Accepting an offer:

  • {% if player.buyer %}Look at the Asks section to see what sellers are offering.{% else %}Look at the Bids section to see what buyers are offering.{% endif %}

  • You may only accept offers—you cannot post your own.
