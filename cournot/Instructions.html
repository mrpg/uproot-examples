You are matched with another participant. You will each independently and simultaneously choose how many units to produce (between 0 and {{ C.MAX_UNITS }}).

The market price is determined by the total number of units both of you produce together:

P  =  100 − Qtotal

Total units (Qtotal) Price (P)
0$100
25$75
50$50
75$25
100$0

Your earnings ($) = Price ($) × your units. There are no production costs.

You may use the simulator to explore different scenarios before making your decision.
